What are Amazon's best selling products?

Amazon is the largest online marketplace in the world. The following visualizations give us an analysis of Amazon's best selling merchandise, and insight into what a vender might want to sell on Amazon to make the most profit.

Bestsellers Scatterplot - video games omitted

This visualization gives us insight into which products have the greatest amount of monthly sales with the least amount of reviews and with video games omitted to normalize the data. Low reviews and high sales is indicative of a niche market that's resonating with customers. A vender might consider selling products with a high sale to review ratio in order to maximize SEO and profits on Amazon.

Bestsellers Barchart

View the top selling product from each category based on monthly unit sales on Amazon. Bestsellers don't necessarily mean best revenue as a product can sell many units but yield lower profits or have a higher sales margin. Revenue numbers give us more insight into the rank of a product.
